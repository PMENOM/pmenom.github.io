About me

I am currently (i.e. 2025 Spring) a graduate student at Università di Padova (Italy, M1), next year I will continue my master degree at Universität Duisburg-Essen (Germany, M2). I'm participating in the program called ALGANT (full scholarship). Information for ALGANT master's students at Padova is HERE, and for Essen is HERE.

I got my undergraduate degree in B.Sc. at the School of Mathematics, Renmin University of China, where I was supervised by Prof. Shanwen Wang. I was also a visiting research student in BICMR, Peking University, and graduated from the program Enhanced Program for Graduate Study there.

My research area is number theory, in particular the representation theory (complex coefficient), trace formula and automorphic forms. [Bump's Book] led me into this field.

I am also studying algebraic geometry/topology, as they offer the necessary language and more perspectives.
