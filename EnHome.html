About me

I am currently (i.e. 2024 Fall) an graduate student at Università di Padova (Italy, M1), next year I will continue my master degree at Universität Duisburg-Essen (Germany, M2). I'm participating in the program called ALGANT. Information for ALGANT master's students at Padova is HERE, and for Essen is HERE.

I got my undergraduate degree in M.Sc. at the School of Mathematics, Renmin University of China, where I was supervised by Prof. Shanwen Wang. I was also a visiting research student in BICMR, Peking University, and graduated from the program Enhanced Program for Graduate Study there.

My research area is number theory, in particular the representation theory and automorphic forms.

I am also interested in Algebraic Geometry, including Complex Geometry and Arithmetic Geometry, and Harmonic analysis. These ‘hobbies’ serve me some estimation techniques from analysis (eg. Langlands classification) and the geometric realization of the abstract objects (eg. Borel-Weil theorem).

Interest: piano, classical music (favourite: Bach, Beethoven, Bruckner, Wagner), swimming (national 2nd-class), badminton, football (favourite team: Arsenal, Argentina, Barça), coffee.
