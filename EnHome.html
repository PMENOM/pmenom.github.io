About me

I am currently an undergraduate student at the School of Mathematics, Renmin University of China, where I am supervised by Prof. Shanwen Wang. I am also a visiting research student in BICMR, Peking University.

My research area is number theory, in particular the representation theory and automorphic forms.

I am also interested in Algebraic Geometry, including Complex Geometry and Arithmetic Geometry, harmonic analysis. These ‘hobby’ serve me some estimation techniques from analysis (eg. Langlands classification) and the geometric realization of the abstract objects (eg. Borel-Weil theorem).

Interest: piano, classical music (favourite: Bach, Beethoven, Bruckner, Wagner), swimming (university team), badminton (school of mathematics team), football (favourite team: Arsenal, Argentina, Barça), coffee.

  • [Notes on Classification with example on GL(2)], 2023.8

    [The Second Part]

    . This is the handwritten note on the representation seminar in 2023 summer. It answers the Local Langlands Correspondence for GL_1 and GL_2, in both archimedean and non-archimedean place. Knowledge on the Galois side is new for us. One Interlude here: Borel Weil Thm (and Beilinson-Bernstein Localization). Then we studied the Langlands Classification and Discrete Series on Arichimedean places with many examples, especially on SL_2(R)\cong SU(1,1), Sp(4,R), and SU(2,2). We gave a specific calculation on the leading term and explained the idea behind the proof. Notes are here:
