About me

I am currently an undergraduate student at the School of Mathematics, Renmin University of China, where I am supervised by Prof. Shanwen Wang.

My research area is number theory, in particular the representation theory and automorphic forms.

I am also interested in complex geometry (especially algebraic geometry perspective), harmonic analysis on R^n (used to be). These ‘hobby’ serve me some estimation techniques from analysis (eg. Langlands classification) and the geometric realization of the abstract objects (eg. Borel-Weil theorem).

Interest: piano, classical music, swimming (university team), badminton (school of mathematics team), football (favourite team: Arsenal, Argentina, Barça), coffee.
